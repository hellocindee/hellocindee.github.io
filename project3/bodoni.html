Bodoni Speciman Poster








The Bodoni type speciman poster was the first typography project I worked on at OTIS.





The Process

The first step in the process was to read the novel "The Bell Jar" and after reading the book I needed to create a metaphor poster for a book reading of the book. The challenge of this project was that the book was dark which made it difficult to read and also made it diffuclt to design a cheerful and welcoming poster that would attract people to attend the event.
