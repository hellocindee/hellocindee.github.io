Mateo's Creamery








I worked on the rebranding for a local ice cream shop called "Mateo's."




The Process

This project was fun to work on because I am passionate about health and I liked the idea of adding an addional tax on drinks that contained any artificial sweetners. I came up with the concept of "Don't drink yourself, fat California," and made a series of ten silk screen print posters.
