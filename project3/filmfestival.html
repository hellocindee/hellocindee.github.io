Soldiers of Fortune








"Soliders of Fortune" was a film festival that I created based on the films Lawless, Food Inc., and Nightcrawler. I designed four posters and shot a film festival trailer.




The Process

The process of this project was researching different films that had the same theme which was harder than I thought. After much research I selected Lawless, Food Inc. and Nightcrawler to be the featured films in my film festival.
