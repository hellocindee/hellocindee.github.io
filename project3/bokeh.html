Bokeh









Bokeh is a publication based on the Psychedelic art movement of the 1980s. I made the publication using mixed media to create bright and colorful compositions.





The Process

This has been my favorite project so far because it was challenging, and I was forced to work out of my comfort zone while using over 10 mediums. This was one of my first projects at OTIS and during the process I didn't think I would ever be able to finish because I felt stuck on the same idea. But after many iterations and compositions I was able to sucessfully complete my "Bokeh" publication.
