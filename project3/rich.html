When it Rains...it Pours.








When it Rains...it Pours, is a typographic book that is based on the word "rich." The book is made up of three different chapters which reflect different types of wealth, the bigger the typography the flashier that type of wealth is.





The Process

Creating the book required a lot of research, finding the right content, binding, size and typefaces. For this book I chose to bind it with a hardcover and book jacket, this type of binding was fitting for thead the concept of wealth.
